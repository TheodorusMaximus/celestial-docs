Project Description

The goal of this project is to develop a web-based e-commerce platform for a small retail company. The platform will allow customers to browse products, make purchases, and manage their accounts.

Objectives

  1. Develop a user-friendly and responsive web application that provides a seamless shopping experience for customers.
  1. Integrate secure payment gateways to ensure the safety of customer transactions.
  1. Build a scalable and maintainable architecture that can accommodate the growth of the company.
  1. Provide robust back-end functionalities for the management of products, orders, and customer data.
  1. Implement an analytics dashboard to track sales and customer behavior, and provide insights for business decisions.

Key Deliverables

  1. A fully functional web application that meets the requirements of the project stakeholders.
  1. A secure payment gateway integration with popular payment methods.
  1. A scalable and maintainable architecture with well-documented code.
  1. A robust back-end system for product and order management, with a user-friendly interface for administrators.
  1. An analytics dashboard with key performance indicators for the business.

Key Stakeholders

  1. Project Manager - responsible for overall project management, monitoring progress, and ensuring that deliverables are met within the agreed timeline.
  1. Development Team - responsible for the design, development, and testing of the platform.
  1. Business Owner - responsible for defining the business requirements, providing feedback, and ensuring that the project meets the business objectives.
  1. Customers - the end-users of the platform, who will provide feedback and suggestions for improvement.

Key Technologies

  1. Front-end: React.js, HTML/CSS, Bootstrap.
  1. Back-end: Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB.
  1. Payment Gateway: Stripe.
  1. Hosting: AWS EC2.
  1. Analytics: Google Analytics.

Timeline

The project is expected to be completed within a 6-month timeline, starting from the date of project initiation.
